# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 23, 2026*

Wanderbound is an open-source photo album generator. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Google account info** — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this to create your account and display your name in the editor.
- **Polarsteps export data** — When you upload your Polarsteps data archive, we process your trips, steps, GPS coordinates, photos, and trip metadata to generate your album.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Session cookie** — We set a single HTTP-only cookie (`session`) to keep you signed in. It expires after 30 days. This is a strictly necessary cookie required for the service to function — we do not use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies.
- **Error reports** — We use [Sentry](https://sentry.io) to collect error reports when something goes wrong. These reports may include your browser type, operating system, and the error details. They do not include your personal content.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google OAuth** — Handles sign-in. Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process.
- **Mapbox** — Provides map tiles for album map pages. When you view a map, your browser requests tiles directly from Mapbox's servers. Mapbox receives the map viewport coordinates but no personal information.
- **Sentry** — Receives error reports for debugging. No personal content is included.

We do not share your data with any other third parties. We do not sell your data. We do not serve ads.

## How We Store Your Data

Your uploaded data (photos, GPS tracks, trip metadata) is stored on the server that runs this instance of Wanderbound. Your data is not shared between instances or with other users.

## Your Rights

You can:

- **Delete your data** — Use the "Delete all my data" option in the editor settings menu. This permanently removes your account, albums, and all uploaded files.
- **Access your data** — Your uploaded Polarsteps archive is the source data. Export features are planned.
- **Correct your data** — Edit your name, locale, and preferences in the editor settings.

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact the instance operator.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Changes will be committed to the project repository.

## Open Source

This project is open source. You can review exactly what data is collected and how it is processed by reading the source code.
